Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts should be typed in English, double-spaced with 1-inch margins.
  • Include an abstract (max 250 words) and 4-6 keywords.
  • Ensure all figures and tables are numbered sequentially and cited in the text.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and submissions are screened.

By proceeding, you agree to these guidelines.
