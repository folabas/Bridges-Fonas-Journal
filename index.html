About the Faculty

Faculty of Natural and Applied Sciences

The Faculty of Natural and Applied Sciences is one of the founding faculties of Lead City University, Ibadan — Nigeria's premier private university celebrating 20 years of academic excellence.

Our scientists are actively collaborating in research and exploring innovative ways of working to improve society. Our primary strategy is ensuring that our research and education activities are mapped onto national and international research priorities.

The faculty contains a number of departments offering various undergraduate and postgraduate courses, including: Biochemistry, Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science, Environmental Management & Toxicology, Microbiology, and Physics.

Submission Guidelines

Manuscript Requirements

  • Document Format

    Submit as Microsoft Word (.docx). Use standard academic formatting with clearly labelled sections: Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussion, and References.

  • Length & Structure

    Typical article length: 4,000–8,000 words excluding references. Include a structured abstract (150–250 words) and 5–7 keywords.

  • Language

    All manuscripts must be written in clear, scholarly English. Manuscripts are screened for plagiarism before peer review.

  • References

    Follow APA 7th Edition referencing style consistently throughout. All in-text citations must appear in the reference list.

  • Review Process

    All manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review. Authors will receive feedback within 4–6 weeks of submission.
