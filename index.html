Submission Guidelines
Document Format

Submit as Microsoft Word (.docx). Use standard academic formatting with clearly labelled sections.

Length & Structure

Typical article length: 4,000–8,000 words. Include a structured abstract and 5–7 keywords.

Language

All manuscripts must be written in clear, scholarly English and screened for plagiarism.

References

Follow APA 7th Edition referencing style consistently throughout the manuscript.

Review Process

All manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review. Feedback within 4–6 weeks.
